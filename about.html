Background

Dr. Gabrielle PT DPT is a physical therapist with a passion for restoring function and independence in clients recovering from stroke, spinal cord injury, and other neurological conditions. After earning a Bachelor's in Physical Therapy degree from University of Santo Tomas, she pursued her Doctorate degree in Physical Therapy from Arcadia University, and is a Certified Stroke Rehab Specialist.

Her clinical approach is grounded in evidence-based practice — combining task-specific training, intensive repetition, and a genuine belief that the nervous system retains the capacity to adapt at every stage of recovery. She has special interest in Spinal Cord Injury rehabilitation, use of Pilates-based exercises in Physical Therapy, and vestibular rehabilitation.

How We Work

WHAT
DRIVES US

01
Clinical Depth

Every session is built on a documented clinical rationale, not a generic protocol. Treatment is delivered 1-on-1 with 50+ minutes of focused care, giving us the time to assess, hypothesize, intervene, and reassess without rushing the session.

02
Progressive Intensity

We don't believe in staying comfortable. Recovery demands progressive challenge — in load, complexity, and environment. We build that progression deliberately and track it session by session.

03
Long-Term Thinking

Traditional PT ends at discharge. STRIVE is built for what comes next — ongoing wellness programming, community reintegration, and the strength to keep improving beyond the episode of care.

What We Provide

CARE OPTIONS
BUILT AROUND REAL LIFE

STRIVE is designed to meet people in the setting that makes the most sense for their goals, their mobility, and the kind of progress they want to make.

01

In-Home Physical Therapy

One-on-one physical therapy delivered in the home for people working toward more independence in daily life, especially when leaving the home is difficult, exhausting, or impractical.

Focused on transfers, walking, balance, endurance, and day-to-day function where it matters most.
Ideal for patients who want skilled PT in their own environment without the burden of clinic travel.
02

Concierge Physical Therapy

Physical therapy that comes to your preferred setting when your goals extend beyond the home and you want treatment in the environment where you actually live, work, and move.

Sessions can be built around spaces like your workplace, a hiking setting, or your preferred gym.
Best for patients who want PT integrated into real-world activities, performance goals, or community mobility.
03

Gym-Based Physical Therapy

Outpatient-style neuro physical therapy using our own gym space for people who need higher-level movement work, progressive loading, and more room to train complex tasks.

Emphasis on lifting, balance, motor planning, gait progression, and building capacity in a structured rehab setting.
A strong fit for patients who are ready for a more intensive clinic-style environment.
04

Wellness Classes

Structured group classes designed to keep people improving beyond traditional rehab through smart, progressive training in a supportive setting.

Classes focus on strength, agility, endurance, balance, and motor planning.
Built for people who want consistency, challenge, and community as part of their long-term recovery and wellness plan.
