Background

Gabrielle is a physical therapist with a passion for restoring function and independence in clients recovering from stroke, spinal cord injury, and other neurological conditions. After earning a Bachelor's in Physical Therapy degree from University of Santo Tomas, she pursued her Doctorate degree in Physical Therapy from Arcadia University, and is a Certified Stroke Rehab Specialist.

Her clinical approach is grounded in evidence-based practice — combining task-specific training, intensive repetition, and a genuine belief that the nervous system retains the capacity to adapt at every stage of recovery. She has special interest in Spinal Cord Injury rehabilitation, use of Pilates-based exercises in Physical Therapy, and vestibular rehabilitation.

How We Work

WHAT
DRIVES US

01
Clinical Depth

Every session is built on a documented clinical rationale — not a generic protocol. We assess, hypothesize, intervene, and reassess with the same rigor used in research settings.

02
Progressive Intensity

We don't believe in staying comfortable. Recovery demands progressive challenge — in load, complexity, and environment. We build that progression deliberately and track it session by session.

03
Long-Term Thinking

Traditional PT ends at discharge. STRIVE is built for what comes next — ongoing wellness programming, community reintegration, and the strength to keep improving beyond the episode of care.
