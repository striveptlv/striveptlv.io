What We Treat

Our Services for Complex Conditions

Delivered 1-on-1 in your Las Vegas home by certified therapists focused exclusively on stroke and vestibular rehabilitation.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Comprehensive recovery for stroke, traumatic brain injury, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and other neurological conditions — designed to restore function, mobility, and independence.

Vestibular Rehabilitation

Focused, evidence-based treatment for BPPV, labyrinthitis, vestibular neuritis, chronic dizziness, and balance disorders — including canalith repositioning maneuvers.

Balance & Fall Prevention

Restore independence in walking, transfers, and daily activities. Working in your actual home ensures gains translate directly to real-world function and safety.

Concussion & Brain Injury

Evidence-based rehabilitation for post-concussion syndrome, including vestibulo-ocular therapy, cognitive-vestibular integration, and graded return-to-activity protocols.

Post-Amputation Rehabilitation

Improve mobility following limb loss, focused on residual limb care, desensitization, strength and balance training, and prosthetic readiness. Emphasizes gait re-education, functional mobility, and progressive return to daily activities with or without a prosthesis.

Home Safety Assessment

A comprehensive evaluation of your Las Vegas home environment with actionable recommendations to reduce fall hazards and improve accessibility — specific to your diagnosis and layout.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a doctor's referral to start PT in Nevada?+
In Nevada, physical therapists can evaluate and treat patients without a physician referral (direct access) for most conditions. For our current cash-pay in-home services, no referral is needed — you can book directly through JaneApp or contact us.
Where in Las Vegas do you provide in-home therapy?+
We serve patients throughout the greater Las Vegas valley including Skye Canyon, Centennial Hills, Summerlin, Spring Valley, and surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm availability in your specific area.
How is STRIVE PT different from a home health agency?+
Home health PT is typically for homebound patients requiring basic functional support. STRIVE PT is for patients seeking higher-intensity neurological and vestibular care in their home — whether they're homebound or simply prefer the privacy and convenience. We provide a level of focused, dedicated clinical care uncommon in traditional home health.
What do CSRS and CBIS certifications mean?+
CSRS (Certified Stroke Rehabilitation Specialist) and CBIS (Certified Brain Injury Specialist) are rigorous national certifications that demonstrate advanced knowledge and clinical competency in neurological rehabilitation. Very few physical therapists in Las Vegas hold these credentials.
Can BPPV be treated in one visit?+
BPPV often responds dramatically to canalith repositioning maneuvers such as the Epley maneuver — many patients experience significant or complete symptom relief in their first session. The number of visits depends on the specific type of BPPV and individual factors assessed during your evaluation.
When will your Las Vegas outpatient clinic open?+
We are actively working toward opening a physical Las Vegas clinic location. Once open, we will accept Medicare Part B. Use the contact form below to join our notification list and we will reach out as soon as the clinic is ready.
How long are sessions and how often will I be seen?+
Initial evaluations are 60–75 minutes. Follow-up sessions are 45–60 minutes. Frequency depends on your diagnosis and goals, which we establish together at evaluation. Because every session is 1-on-1, every minute is active, high-quality treatment time — not time spent waiting or working with an aide.
Can I use my HSA or FSA card?+
Yes — physical therapy is an eligible HSA/FSA expense. We also provide detailed superbills (itemized receipts with diagnosis and procedure codes) that you can submit to your insurance for potential out-of-network reimbursement, depending on your plan's benefits.
