SCI Rehabilitation · Las Vegas, NV

Strength. Confidence. Independence.

Spinal Cord Injury Rehabilitation

Post spinal cord injury, the work is hard and the stakes are real. We work with both complete and incomplete injuries, building programs around two things: building meaningful strength and expanding your independence — wherever that work happens best, whether that's your home or our movement gym.

Request a Consultation Our Approach
What Drives Every Session

Two Goals.
Zero Compromises.

Pillar One

Growing Independence

Independence isn't a milestone — it's the mission. Every session is oriented around the functional tasks that define your daily life: transfers, mobility, self-care, and community participation. We work backward from the life you want to live.

Pillar Two

Post-SCI Strengthening

Strength is the foundation of everything else. We target the muscle groups that matter for your level of injury, building capacity safely and progressively — with room to keep going as you get stronger.

Areas of Focus

What We Work On

Every program is built around your injury level, your residual function, and your goals — not a standard protocol.

Upper Body Strength

Targeted resistance training for functional pulling, pushing, and weight-bearing — critical for transfers and mobility.

Transfer Training

Bed-to-chair, floor-to-chair, car transfers — practiced safely with progressive independence as the goal.

Wheelchair Mobility

Propulsion efficiency, pressure relief, and navigating real-world surfaces and slopes in your home and community.

Standing & Gait (where applicable)

For partial injuries, we build toward standing tolerance, assisted walking, and functional ambulation with appropriate support.

Core & Trunk Control

Seated stability, postural endurance, and trunk strength support a wide range of functional skills we work toward.

Cardio & Endurance

For higher-level injuries, overall cardiovascular endurance and conditioning are part of the plan.

The Process

Simple. Direct. Effective.

No long intake. No waiting. We get moving fast — with a plan that is built to evolve as you do.

01

We assess your strength and function where it counts. At home or at our movement gym inside Shinkyokushin Karate Las Vegas on Saturdays — wherever makes sense for you.

03

Your Program

A custom plan targeting your priority movements, strength gaps, and independence goals.

04

Keep Pushing

We progress the program continuously. Harder when you're ready. Adjusted when you're not.
